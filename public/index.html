What is Voto?

Voto is a quick and easy way to connect with your students. With its simple UI and cross-platform use, Voto is the polling software of the future.

With premade sessions or spontaneous questions you can quickly judge your classroom's understanding of any topic at any time.

About Us

Extra devices - Painful Setup, where we are today, we can do better. Here at Voto we are striving to create an all purpose polling system which will be easy-to-use, quick to setup, and available across all platforms.
