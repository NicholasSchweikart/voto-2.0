What we do

We enable teachers everywhere
to quickly engage their students

Voto is a quick and easy way to connect with your students. With its simple UI and
cross-platform use, Voto is the pulling software of the future!

With premade sessions or spontaneous questions you can quickly judge your classroom's
understanding of any topic at any time.

Instantly Join

No download required! Simply enter a link and be able to participate in live polling sessions.

Images Transfer

No projector? Our software will transfer any image associated with the question to the student's device!

Setup Anywhere

This software can be accessed from anywhere! Setup questions at home and then use it in class!

Cross platform

As a responsive, web-based app, this software will work on any modern, web-capable device.

Get Maxium Response in the Classroom with our software.

With a multitude of different options for you to connect with your students, you will be able to judge exactly how well your classroom is following with a select topic. This can be done with

  • Multiple Choice
  • True / False
  • Text Response
  • Quizzes
